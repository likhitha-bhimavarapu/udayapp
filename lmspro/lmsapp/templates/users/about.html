Our Story

LinkMeIn is a premier Training, Development, and Software Services company based in Hyderabad, India.

Founded in 2010, we have been at the forefront of delivering comprehensive Application Development & Managed Services, E-Commerce Solutions, BPO services, and Professional Training & Consulting Services. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

From maintaining existing applications to full life-cycle development of enterprise-wide business solutions, our technical teams across India deliver high-value solutions that drive business success.
